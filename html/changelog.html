01 January 2021

Arkatos updated:

  • Fixed a case where a syndicate assault pod was unlaunchable.

Gandalf2k15 updated:

  • Internet sounds.

NotRanged updated:

  • Ported over the hardened soles positive quirk from the old codebase.

SkyratBot updated:

  • shotgun slug damage increased
  • cats are skinnier
  • Cancelling a vote now leaves a log and message.
  • You can once again put oars into lava boats in order to use them to cross lava.
  • cleaning decals in general
  • The cursed hot spring no longer polymorphs incorporeal critters such as unrevealed revenants.
  • Pancakes now griddle to be cooked
  • fixed vibebot connectivity issues

coiax updated:

  • Tom, the legendary prison mouse, no longer fears chewing electrified wires.

31 December 2020

Azarak updated:

  • Genetics can no longer unlock thermal vision

Dex updated:

  • Added the missing console to the Arena shuttle

Gandalf2k15 updated:

  • You can now flip tables by ctrl+shift+clicking them!
  • Turret IDS

Gandalf2k15, Avunia updated:

  • You can no longer spam certain emotes.
  • You can't clap without hands anymore
  • You can clap without a mouth again
  • Emotes now have a global cooldown instead per emote
  • Emote volume reduced from 50% to 25%
  • Emote cooldown increased to 15 seconds
  • Uncooked the spaghetti that *scream was, among others
  • Emotes can now have variable volume adjustments in the code

NotRanged updated:

  • Added old skyrat's food like/dislike system to preferences.
  • Increased the base chance for a supermatter to spawn anomaly-core dropping anomalies to 25%.

SkyratBot updated:

  • map generation will properly clean flora and rocks where it should.
  • Cut AI Wires maphelper for airlocks.
  • AI wires on the Space Pirate shuttle are cut by default.

coiax updated:

  • You may now pick Implant as a preferred uplink location, meaning that if you become a traitor, you will start with an uplink implant. The cost of the implant (4 TC) is deducted from your total starting TC, because the Syndicate doesn't give freebies.
  • Admins removing an uplink from a person with an uplink implant will now destroy the implant, rather than leaving an implant that does nothing.

30 December 2020

Melbert updated:

  • New negative quirk: 'Bad Touch'. With it, you gain a negative moodlet from being hugged instead of a positive one, and you gain a negative moodlet from headpats and grabs.
  • blind people can now feel headpats

Nari Harimoto updated:

  • Kilo Station white ship shuttle dock no longer spawns a redundant mining shuttle

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Dead bodies with a brain but no ghost actively inside the body will once again appear dead when examined
  • The clown car can once again be driven
  • Mechs can once again open doors by bumping into them (assuming the rider has access, obviously)

SkyratBot updated:

  • The B.E.P.I.S market has been stabilized
  • You are no longer able to teleport rigged power cells across the station to remotely detonate departments using the BRPED. Rigged cells will instead explode inside the BRPED when you attempt to transfer them. That's right, you can now rig BRPEDs to explode when people use them.
  • Fixed Space Dragon's overlay not being set properly on death
  • Fixed Space Dragon's color selection not limiting darker colors as it should
  • Replaced US Indigenous People's Day with it's international equivalent
  • Fixed zero damage items with set attack verbs not showing chat messages anyway.
  • jitteriness, melee attack animations and resting/standing up should no longer momentarily disable the floating movetype.
  • PubbyStation subtypes of booze-o-mat and wallmed
  • ERT shuttle access on Kilostation. Interns and other livestock may board again.
  • Misplaced solar wires on Kilo have been corrected
  • Some more minor decals/grille placement on Kilo
  • Spiders will no longer spawn in space on Kilo
  • The vacant offices on Ice Box and Delta are now public access.
  • The Ice Box vacant office airlock has been turned into a plain airlock to reflect the fact it no longer needs engineering access to open. It has also been renamed to reflect the fact that Vacant Office B no longer exists.
  • The Meta vacant office airlock has a name now when it was unnamed before.

coiax updated:

  • The "eye of god" only becomes unremovable when put in your eye.

29 December 2020
